What is Build Me Blocks?

Growth, determination, creativity, battles, challenges, disappointments are all part of life, an adult life and a child’s life. Build ME Blocks was created to help face those battles, challenges and disappointments so that growth, determination and creativity are able to thrive in a child. The focus of Build ME Blocks is to take a child’s world and make it a learning experience without them by incorporating their everyday experiences into a learning opportunity. The concepts learned become a part of who they are and continue with them through adolescence and into adulthood. In Build ME Blocks, the M of ME represents motivation and The E represents Education. Motivation is learned and encouraged through the Special Agent Mission Interactive Program. Education is learned and encouraged through interactive activities that bring the child’s world to life. Reading, Writing, Math and Science activities are encouraged. Many resource sites, books and favorite activity products are suggested to help you and your child focus on education. Educational activities are encouraged in the Special Agent Mission program also.
